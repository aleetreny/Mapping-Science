\section{Deprecated Draft: Exploratory Morphological Typologies}

This chapter draft is not included by \texttt{memory/memoria.tex}. The active
static typology uses Ward hierarchical clustering, Euclidean distance, robust
median/IQR scaling, and \(k=5\) over the eight structural morphology metrics.
The rebuilt silhouette is \(0.174\), and the selected static group sizes are
11, 40, 44, 64, and 82 subfields.

Dynamic clustering is based on standardized slopes of the same eight structural
metrics across five temporal windows. The selected temporal solution remains a
descriptive summary of movement patterns rather than a natural taxonomy.
