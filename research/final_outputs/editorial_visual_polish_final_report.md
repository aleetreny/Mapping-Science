# Editorial and Visual Polish Final Report

## 1. Audit summary

The audit found that the thesis was scientifically complete but rhythmically repetitive. The main repeated concepts were OpenAlex as pragmatic taxonomy, SPECTER2 as learned representation, UMAP/PCA/PCoA as visualization only, metrics computed in original embedding space, clusters as descriptive typologies, and morphology as not quality, impact, productivity, or causality. These cautions remain, but later reminders were shortened.
